Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase your products?

+

Our products are available through our CSA program, at local farmers markets, and select retail partners. Contact us for specific availability information.

What is your CSA program?

+

Our Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program provides members with a weekly box of seasonal produce. Members pay upfront for a season and receive fresh, organic produce throughout the growing period.

Do you offer farm tours?

+

Yes, we offer guided farm tours by appointment. Tours are available for individuals, families, schools, and groups. Please contact us to schedule a tour.

What sustainable practices do you employ?

+

We use various sustainable practices including crop rotation, cover cropping, integrated pest management, water conservation, composting, and renewable energy sources.

How can I get involved with your farm?

+

There are several ways to get involved: join our CSA program, volunteer on the farm, attend our workshops and events, or partner with us for educational initiatives.
